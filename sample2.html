Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an online prescription?

Getting an online prescription is easy. Simply fill out our online form, provide your medical details, and our doctors will review your case and issue the prescription digitally.

Can I get a prescription for controlled substances online?

No, we cannot issue prescriptions for controlled substances online. You will need to visit a physical clinic for such prescriptions.

Are online prescriptions legal and valid?

Yes, online prescriptions are legal and valid in most cases. Our doctors are licensed and follow all the necessary regulations to issue digital prescriptions.
