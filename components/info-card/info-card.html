Medications are important tools that can be used to treat various diseases or relieve symptoms. These substances are typically prescribed by doctors and are used as part of a specific treatment plan. Proper and prescribed use of medications is essential, as misuse or overdose can lead to serious health problems. Adhering to the medication's instructions is critical for effective treatment. Unnecessary or excessive use of medications can lead to unwanted side effects and even poisoning. Therefore, it is important to always follow the dosage and frequency of use recommended by your doctor. Safe use of medications will help to effectively manage health problems.
